Bluetti Portable Power Station

Off-grid living with Bluetti

2023-2024

Bluetti portable power stations are the ideal appliance If you're going off grid, providing enough power to keep your appliances or devices operating. I used the Bluetti AC180 portable power station for up to 12 months on my travels across Europe capturing authentic content of it in use and showing its benefits for off-grid living in a campervan. How many can say they were able to live off grid in the Sahara desert for a week thanks to the Bluetti portable power station!

I captured hundreds of photos of the Bluetti product in use, you can see some of my favourite below. I also created a reel to showcase the Bluetti AC180 to my audience. My aim with this reel was to grab people's attention by comparing my dog Sandy to the Bluetti AC180. In the video, most viewers will think that I am reffering to Sandy, only for me to switch to the Bluetti half way through. This reel is a perfect example of how I like to make original content that stands out, I did this by creating a unique voiceover and showing off the Bluetti with my videography skills.
