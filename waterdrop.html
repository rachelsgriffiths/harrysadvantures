Waterdrop Straw Filter

Promoting the Waterdrop Straw Filter in action

2023

The filter straw is a compact water filter that provides safe drinking water. It's suitable for the outdoors hiking adding camping, making it a perfect product for my lifestyle while travelling when I can't be sure that water is safe to drink.

Waterdrop were aware of my plans to travel Europe in my campervan, they requested for me to use their Straw Filter on my travels and create videos to show off different features of the product. In the first video they wanted me to highlight how to use the Life Straw and Gravity Bag, demonstrating how you can safely turn dirty water into safe drinking water. The objective of the second video was to focus on the compact size and ease of travelling with the product. I did this by taking the Life Straw on a hike and showing how easy it is to replenish your water if you ever run out.
