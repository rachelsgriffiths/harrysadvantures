Scrubba Wash Bag

Promoting the Scrubba wash bag to my Instagram audience

2023-2024

The Scrubba washbag is an innovative portable washbag ideal for travellers. Scrubba needed an influencer collaboration and authentic content of their product to increase awareness and reach as many new accounts as possible.

I was approached to work with them to produce multiple videos to post on my social media channels. This included instructional videos where I demonstrated how to use the product as well as more natural content where I simply showed the benefits of the Scrubba washbag in daily life while travelling, such as being able to wash clothes anywhere at any time and avoiding wasting time in a laundrette.
