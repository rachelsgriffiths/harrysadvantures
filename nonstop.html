Ambassador role with Non-stop Dogwear

2023-2024

Non-stop dogwear develops, designs, and sells dog equipment worldwide with the ultimate purpose to "Release your dog's potential." I have an ambassador role, well Sandy does, showing off Non-stop Dogwear products in use on our travels. This ranges from leads to life-jackets, which I have photographed all across Europe.

For example, you can see Sandy modelling her life-jacket on an Arctic beach in Norway, rocking her gorgeous green harness in the Sahara and using her coat on a cold morning in Turkey.
