Tractive GPS Pet Tracker

Collaborating with Tractive on their social media campaigns

2023-2024

The Tractive GPS Pet Tracker is an innovative device designed to track your dog's movements, monitor their health, and record their activity. I had the opportunity to collaborate with Tractive on three of their social media campaigns, showcasing their product to my audience.

Each campaign had a unique focus, allowing me to highlight different features and benefits of the product. I was provided with a brief for each campaign and key points to focus on to raise awareness. Through these reels, I demonstrated my ability to work effectively to a brief while using my storytelling skills to create engaging and unique videos.
